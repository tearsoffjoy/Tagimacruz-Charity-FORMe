Promotion of Lumad Culture: Speaking with them

The Lumad community, a mindanao indigenous group continues to fight for what is essentially human rights, this project aims to:
1. preserve and promote the rich and diverse tradtions and culture of the lumad people
2. providing their needs and helping them to battle discrimination and inequality.
3. Start an outreach programs to create a bridge to deliver the goal of supporting the said community.
